09/1/10: Shape - Artillery

While the graphical effects of this demo are absolutely mind blowing, the real attraction that makes this demo so phenomenal is the SID tune. Composed by Geir Tjelta of the group Maniacs of Noise, this tune really goes above and beyond in demonstrating the power and capabilities of the MOS 6581 sound chip.
